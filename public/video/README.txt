Place your video files and poster images in this folder.

Required video file names used by the website:
- vfx-showreel.mp4
- project-01.mp4
- project-02.mp4
- project-03.mp4

Required poster image names:
- vfx-poster.jpg
- project-01-poster.jpg
- project-02-poster.jpg
- project-03-poster.jpg

You can also use external video links.
Open src/data.js and replace any video path with a YouTube, Vimeo, Google Drive, or direct hosted video URL.
